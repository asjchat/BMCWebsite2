About Us

We provide enriching environments to help young people discover what they love, what they are good at, and how to match their talent with economic opportunity to thrive and flourish in all areas of their life.

At our core, we are a creator camp looking for students who believe in learning by doing.

OUR CURRICULUM

Week 1: Education and Wealth Creation

Students will meet with business leaders, tour company facilities, and explore the Tampa Bay Area's vibrant corporate world. 

Week 2: The Science of Happiness 

Students will meet with globally renowned thinkers and will discover what it means to be happy. 

Week 3: Showing Your Work 

Students will take what they have learned and show it to their families, friends, and the world.
