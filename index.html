About Us

Kadoka Academy encourages students to explore fundamental questions while developing habits of mind that will prepare them for success in all areas of life. Students are asked to reach their own conclusions through deep thinking, critical analysis, and intense discussion. Some refer to Kadoka Academy’s Summer Program as Adulting 101, a creative camp for builders, or a platform to discuss all those things we wish we learned when we were in school.
