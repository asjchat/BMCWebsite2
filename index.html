Course Curriculum

Money, Economics and Wealth

Getting reach without being lucky, personal finance 101, positive vs. zero sum games. budgeting taxes, and retirement accounts, money traps, borrowing and loans, home ownership, interest rates, compensation of creative work. 

Wisdom and Virtue

Honesty, integrity and telling the truth, the austerity for the modern age. What is the good life and how do I live it? Perspectives on the meaning of life. What Can I do to increase the odds of flourishing over time?

Relationships, Social, and Emotional Intelligence

Long term games with long term people, becoming more mature, how to not make major blunders in life, maps and models of consciousness. 


Influences: Daniel Gilbert, Jonathan Haidt, Ken Wilber, Laura Schlessinger, Richard Schwartz, James Fowler, Mr. Rogers, M. Scott Peck

Books: The Obstacle is the Way, The Ego is the Enemy, Social Intelligence, No Bad Parts, The Road Less Traveled

Science, Statistics, and Bias

The difference between correlation and causation, empirical generalization, is science absolute? What is group think? What are the consequences for a public that does not understand statistics and how does it lead to populist policies? What is the Hawthorne effect?


Influences: Richard Feynman, Nassim Taleb, Tyler Cowen, Robert Cialdini, Dan Ariely


Books: Surely Youâ€™re Joking Mr. Feynman, The Black Swan, Fooled by Randomness, Predictably Irrational, Pre-Suasion, Influence

College

Getting into the best universities, what is the best way to earn money and gain status? (If that is your goal) Perspectives and stories on college admissions. Making the most out of college and financing. 


Influences: Peter Thiel, Anya, Seth Godin, Micheal Ellsberg, Kyle Eschenroeder, Surenda Mitra

Books: The Last Safe Investment, Education of Millionaires

Finding your Strengths

Playing to your childhood talents, understanding yourself and others, strength based approach (moving from strength to strength.) 



Influences: Joseph Campbell, Caroline Myss, Richard Rohr


Books: Archetypes: Who Are You?, The Heroâ€™s Journey, StrengthsFinder2.0

Career and Professionalism 

How do you get clout? Building relationships and reputation outside of school. Identifying and developing your own charisma and connecting it with your profession. Should you pursue your passion or do what is practical? 


Influences: Cal Newport, Tyler Cowen, Seth Godin, Albert Schweitzer


Books: Answering the Call, Deep Work, So Good They Cant Ignore You: Why Skills Trump Passion, How to Fail at Everything and Still Win Big


Health, Wellness, and the Science of Happiness

Everyone wants to be fit and healthy but few achieve itâ€¦why? What can we do to be top 1%? Habit forming behaviors, diet, nutrition, athletics. Longevity science, blue zones, youth, appearance. Fashion, does it matter? How to dress to increase confidence, influence, respect, and how it      impacts outcomes personally and professionally, according to the latest research.


Influences: Kelly McGonigal, Richard Branson, Dr. Andrew Weil, Dr. Mark Hyman, Swami Rama, Tim Ferriss, Richard Branson, Tony Robbins, Wim Hof, Michael Pollen, Paul Stamets, Andrew Huberman, Peter Attia



Books: UltraPrevention, Relaxation Response, Pantanjali Yoga Aphorisms, 4 Hour Body, Why Zebras Dont Get Ulcers
